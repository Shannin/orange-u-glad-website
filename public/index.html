Consuming this product causes THC to be absorbed sublingually and though ingestion. The amount of time before you start to feel the effects of the THC after consumption can range from 30 minutes to two hours, and is dependent on a number of factors from how much food you’ve ate to your metabolic rate. Wait at least one hour after initial consumption before increasing dosage.

It’s important to know your dose when medicating with cannabis. We recommend starting with half a slice (5mg) and waiting at least one hour before consuming more. Start slow and low, and work your way up until you find a dose that works for you.
